Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my faovrite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  • Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  • Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  • Once butter has melted and cream and eggs
  • Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds(as if you were making an omelette)
  • When te eggs re still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
